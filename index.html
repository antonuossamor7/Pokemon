Arena

In this graph, you can observe the weakness of a Pokemon compared to another one by using their power. There are 18 different powers. Those who are interesting in our case are Normal, Fire, Water, Electric, Grass, Poison, Flying, Bug and Fairy power. If you click on one of the power, you are going to see a path to another power. Then three colors are going to show the damage of the power against to power it is linked to.

  • If the path is YELLOW, the power takes double the damage
  • If the path is BLUE, the power takes half the damage
  • If the path is PURPLE, the power is imuned
